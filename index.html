How this is calculated

Reading a test is not the same as learning from it.

A readable test tells you which ad won this week. It does not tell you why it won, which angle to push next, or what three months of tests have already ruled out. That is a learning system, and it is the difference between creative that compounds and creative that starts from zero every campaign.

The numbers above are the first page of that system. The rest is finding where your funnel leaks the margin this calculator just measured, and running tests that feed every result back into the next brief.
